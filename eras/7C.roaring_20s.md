## Causes
- Low post-war morale
- Increased economy from allies' trades

## Effects
- Increased the federal government's civil involvement
    - "Safety Net"
    - Intervene economy
    - Promote general welfare
    - Deliver public services

## Movements
- Harlem Renaissance
- Science v. Fundamentalism
- Consumerism
- Nativism
- Red Scare
